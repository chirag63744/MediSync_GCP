Our AI uses a 3-step decision cycle to ensure the fastest, most effective response.

01.

Smart Allocation

Based on real-time location and availability, the AI dispatches the nearest and most appropriate ambulance.

02.

In-Transit Synthesis

Live patient vitals and historical data are synthesized into a "Critical Condition Summary" for the receiving hospital.

03.

Intelligent Matching

The AI identifies the best-equipped facility based on the patient's summary and live hospital capabilities.
